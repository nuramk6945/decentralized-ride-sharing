# Ride Coordinator Smart Contract

## Summary
Decentralized peer-to-peer ride sharing platform that eliminates centralized platform fees through direct driver-rider matching and smart contract-based payment handling.

## Features
- **P2P Ride Matching**: Direct connection between riders and drivers
- **Smart Contract Escrow**: Secure payment handling with automatic release
- **Geolocation Coordination**: Pickup and destination coordinate management
- **Status Tracking**: Complete ride lifecycle management
- **Zero Platform Fees**: Direct payments without intermediaries

## Impact
Revolutionizes transportation by removing platform intermediaries, resulting in higher driver earnings and lower rider costs while maintaining security through blockchain technology.
